Customer Churn Prediction Model

Analyzed customer churn data for a telephone and internet service company containing 20 different features.

Built and tuned 5 different classification models using the sklearn library to predict likelihood of customer churn as well as which factors were causing it with an accuracy of 80%.

Insights:

  • Customer tenure and the companies fiber optic internet service were the biggest reasons for churn.
  • New customers are more likely to churn.
  • DSL internet service led to higher customer satisfaction.

Medical Insurance Cost Estimator

Aimed to predict medical insurance costs in the United States based on 6 factors using a simulated dataset based on U.S Census Data.

Built and tuned 5 different regression models with grid searches using the Sci-Kit Learn library and subsequently built an app using Streamlit for getting a cost estimation. Predicted costs with an accuracy of 90%.

Insights:

  • Smoker status and age were the biggest factors in determining insurance costs.
  • Americans in the southeast region of the country pay more for healthcare.
  • Men pay slightly more than women for healthcare.
